# Strator AI

AI-powered claims auditing for Indonesian health insurers.

## The Problem

Indonesia's health insurance industry loses **Rp 15T+ annually** to claims leakage. 5–10% of all claims contain fraud, waste, or abuse — yet most insurers manually review less than 2% of claims. The rest slip through unchecked.

Traditional TPAs rely on random sampling and manual review: slow, expensive, and full of blind spots.

## What Strator Does

Strator audits **100% of claims** in under 2 minutes each. The platform ingests claims from any source, extracts structured data with AI, flags fraud and overcharges, and generates actionable recommendations — replacing random spot-checks with comprehensive, automated auditing.

**Core pipeline:** Secure Intake → AI Extraction → Issue Detection → AI Recommendation → Analyst Workbench → Portfolio Insights

## Key Metrics

- **40%** leakage reduction
- **<2 min** per claim review
- **100%** claims audited (vs <2% industry average)
